AKAN NAMES

Akan is basically a native language from Ghana formerly known as the Gold coast.Akan means first, foremost, indicating the enlightened and civilized,
They also name children on the day they are born respectively to their Akan names.

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
