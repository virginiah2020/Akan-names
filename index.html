What's my Akan name

Akan is basically a native language from Ghana formerly known as the Gold coast.Akan means first, foremost, indicating the enlightened and civilized,They also name children on the day they are born respectively to their Akan names.People in Ghana are named depending on their gender.Below is the names of the Akan names both male and females.Automatically when you enter the day you were born.you will know your Akan name.

Day.of.week Male Female
Sunday Kwasi Akosua
Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
Wednesday Kwaku Akua
Thursday Yaw Yaa
Friday Kofi Afua
Saturday Kwame Ama
